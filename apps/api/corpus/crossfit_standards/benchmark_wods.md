# CrossFit Benchmark WODs

## Fran

21-15-9 reps of Thrusters (95/65 lb) and Pull-ups. One of the most iconic benchmark WODs. Elite times are sub-2:00 for men, sub-3:00 for women. Fran tests both aerobic capacity and upper-body pulling strength under fatigue.

## Grace

30 Clean and Jerks for time (135/95 lb). Tests barbell cycling efficiency and shoulder strength. Elite time is sub-1:30. Pacing strategy matters — unbroken vs. small sets.

## Helen

3 rounds for time: 400m run, 21 KB swings (53/35 lb), 12 pull-ups. A classic triplet testing aerobic base, hip hinge strength, and upper body endurance. Target time: 9-12 minutes for intermediate athletes.

## Cindy

AMRAP 20: 5 pull-ups, 10 push-ups, 15 air squats. Tests raw gymnastics endurance. Score = total rounds. Good baseline: 15+ rounds. Elite: 30+ rounds.

## Diane

21-15-9: Deadlifts (225/155 lb) and Handstand Push-ups. Tests posterior chain strength and overhead pressing. Sub-3:00 is elite level.

## Annie

50-40-30-20-10 reps of Double-unders and Sit-ups. Tests jump rope skill, core endurance, and pacing under aerobic stress.

## Barbara

5 rounds for time (rest 3 min between rounds): 20 pull-ups, 30 push-ups, 40 sit-ups, 50 air squats. A high-volume bodyweight test.

## Murph

1 mile run, 100 pull-ups, 200 push-ups, 300 squats, 1 mile run (with 20 lb vest). Memorial WOD. Tests broad fitness and mental toughness.

## Isabel

30 Snatches for time (135/95 lb). Tests barbell cycling technique and overhead strength at speed.

## Jackie

For time: 1000m row, 50 thrusters (45/35 lb), 30 pull-ups. Tests aerobic capacity, cycling under moderate load, and pulling endurance.
